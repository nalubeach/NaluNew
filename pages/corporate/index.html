Main Dining Room

Our main dining room combines vibrant energy with sophisticated design, accommodating up to 150 guests for seated dinners or 250 for standing receptions. Perfect for large corporate gatherings that require an impressive setting.

  • Capacity: 150 seated, 250 standing
  • Features: Full restaurant buyout, AV equipment available, customizable layouts
  • Best for: Large corporate events, holiday parties, product launches

The Lounge

Our elegant lounge offers an intimate setting with plush seating and a more relaxed atmosphere. Ideal for networking events, informal meetings, or pre-dinner receptions.

  • Capacity: 60 seated, 100 standing
  • Features: Private bar, flexible seating arrangements, ambient lighting
  • Best for: Networking events, casual team gatherings, cocktail receptions

Private Dining Room

Our exclusive private dining room offers a secluded sanctuary for more intimate corporate events. With its own dedicated service team, it provides privacy and personalized attention.

  • Capacity: 30 seated, 45 standing
  • Features: Complete privacy, dedicated staff, bespoke menus
  • Best for: Board meetings, client dinners, executive lunches

Jungle Terrace

Our stunning outdoor terrace creates a tropical oasis in the heart of London. Surrounded by lush greenery, it's perfect for summer events and offers a unique setting for memorable corporate gatherings.

  • Capacity: 40 seated, 70 standing
  • Features: Retractable roof, heating, tropical ambiance
  • Best for: Summer events, product launches, press days
